AMERICAN TOURISTER BRAND

VALVE TRUCKS US BRANDS WITH PRICE 1 DOLLAR

The story begins in 1933, when Sol Koffler decided to put all of his savings to start a suitcase company in Providence, Rhode Island, USA. His dream is "A suitcase can be sold for 1 USD".

VALIES TRAVEL AMERICAN TOURISTER & STAY ROAD HAD

- In 1945, air travel began to flourish, a suitcase with a lighter weight to accommodate air travel became in high demand. The American Tourister immediately launched a lighter, easier-to-pack briefcase. Mark a start for quick and optimal response to your needs.

- In the 1950s, found a company testing new materials to create the world's first hard case model.

- In the 1960s, International Travel skyrocketed, American Tourister was the first company to equip crew bags for test flights of major airlines.

- In the 80s and 90s, a complete line of suitcases was born with many features to meet the needs of the times. At the same time, the durability of the plastic suitcase is verified by the image of Gorilla monkey stepping on the suitcase. New developments in functionality have surpassed market expectations and surpassed even the most demanding critics.

- In 1993, American Tourister officially joined the Samsonite family, inheriting all the quintessence, the most advanced technology. Together with Samsonite, a common ideal is to bring the best and the most convenient for travelers.

AMERICAN TOURISTER VALUES VALUES

The American Tourister suitcase brand is a combination of many travel elements including style and confidence. Therefore, American Tourister is committed to ensuring that all products are durable, lightweight, fashionable, multifunctional and cost-effective. That's why American Tourister has been a trusted brand worldwide for more than 84 years and is now available in more than 100 countries!
