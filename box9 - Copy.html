The iconic brand from France - Delsey has more than 7 decades of continuous development, gaining over 70 international awards and patents.

Founded in 1946, the brand name is a combination of DELAHAYE, a prestigious enterprise in the production of camera bags and photo equipment, with the SEYNHAEVE brothers, aiming to create products that contain onions. The reason is creative, trendy and handy for travelers.

Considered to be the world's leading brand of suitcases, backpacks, bags and travel accessories, Delsey has been officially present in more than 110 countries on 5 continents so far.
