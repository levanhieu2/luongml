development and development history balo High Sierra

Founded in 1978 by father Herb and son Hank Bernbaum in Chicago, USA. Inspired by the highest mountain, tallest ridges and largest waterfalls as a reference frame and the goal that High Sierra aims to.

In any journey, whether high mountains or deep sea, whether in work or life, accompany you - that's the High Sierra.
